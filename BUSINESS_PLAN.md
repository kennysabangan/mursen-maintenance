# Mursen Maintenance — Business Plan & Offer Strategy

> **Last Updated:** 2026-05-01
> **Competitive Research Scope:** Covington, KY + 20mi radius (Northern KY / Cincinnati metro)
> **Services:** Lawn Care, Landscaping, Window Cleaning, Power Washing, General Handyman/Maintenance
> **Target Markets:** Rental property owners/landlords + homeowners

---

## 1. Why Mursen Exists

The founders operate **20+ rental units** in the Covington/NKY area. They know firsthand that rental property maintenance is fragmented, expensive, and time-consuming:
- Chasing 5 different contractors for one property
- Surprise $800 plumber bills that eat into cash flow
- Emergency repairs at 2am because deferred maintenance became a crisis
- Coordinating schedules between tenants, vendors, and owners
- Not knowing what maintenance actually costs per unit

Mursen Maintenance solves this by being the **one subscription** that covers both interior handyman work AND exterior services. One team, one bill, zero chasing.

- Charge **$100-200/unit flat fee** OR **8-12% of monthly rent**
- They *coordinate* maintenance but rarely *do* it — they mark up contractor bills
- Landlords still deal with tenant complaints, scheduling, and surprise costs

### Landlord Maintenance Budget Reality
- **Handyman on retainer:** $100-400/unit/month (BiggerPockets)
- **Property manager fees:** $100-200/unit flat fee OR 8-12% of rent
- **Total maintenance budget:** ~$1.00-1.30/sq ft annually
- **Emergency repairs:** 32% of all repair costs are emergency (preventable with proactive maintenance)

## 5. Hormozi-Style Offer Structure

### Tier 1: "Exterior Essentials" — $89/month

**Included:**
- Weekly/bi-weekly mowing + edging + blowing
- 2x/year window cleaning (inside + out)
- 1x/year exterior power wash (siding/driveway/patio up to 1,750 sq ft)
- Quarterly gutter cleaning
- Seasonal leaf/yard debris cleanup

**Bonus:** Free curb appeal assessment on signup ($150 value)
**Guarantee:** If your property doesn't look noticeably better in 30 days, first month refunded

---

### Tier 2: "Total Property Shield" — $179/month ⭐ Recommended / Flagship

**Everything in Tier 1, PLUS:**
- Monthly landscaping refresh (mulch, pruning, seasonal color)
- 4x/year window cleaning
- 2x/year power wash (spring/deep clean + fall)
- ALL handyman services covered:
  - Plumbing (clogs, leaky faucets, toilets, sinks)
  - Locks/deadbolts (install, rekey, repair)
  - Light electrical (outlets, fixtures, fans)
  - Basic carpentry (shelves, doors, trim)
  - Drywall patching / painting touch-ups
- Priority 48-hour response
- No trip charges, ever

**Bonus:** Free driveway sealant after 6 months ($350 value)
**Guarantee:** Any handyman issue resolved within 48 hours or it's free

---

### Tier 3: "Portfolio Partner" — $349/month (Multi-Unit / Landlord)

**Everything in Tier 2, PLUS:**
- Dedicated maintenance lead assigned to your portfolio
- Monthly property walkthrough + inspection report
- 24-hour emergency response (not 48)
- Replacement coordination (appliances, flooring when needed)
- Tenant move-in/move-out turnaround service
- Discounted unit rate for 3+ properties

**Bonus:** Free property condition report ($500 value)
**Guarantee:** We handle every maintenance issue. If a tenant complaint ever reaches you about maintenance, that month's fee is waived.
