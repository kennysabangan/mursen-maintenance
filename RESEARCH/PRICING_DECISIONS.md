---

## Key Pricing Decisions

1. **Home Care raised from $349 to $479.** Original $349 with 8 hrs handyman = ~$890 of à la carte value. Not sustainable. New $479 with 4 hr cap is margin-positive.

2. **Lawn Plus ($109) added.** Captures à la carte mow customers who won't jump to $199 Starter. Funnel gap fix.

3. **Handyman Plus ($149) added.** Captures customers who don't need lawn but want a regular fix-it person. Opposite funnel capture.

4. **Property Manager scope rewritten.** Rental-unit optimized, not homeowner-equivalent. Excludes homeowner-style project work, custom requests, owner-occupied amenity services.

5. **Seasonal scope document required.** Solves "why am I paying $479 in February" cancellation problem before it starts.
