# 5 Seasonal Maintenance Tasks Every Property Owner Should Know

Regular seasonal maintenance isn't just about keeping your property looking good — it's a critical part of protecting your investment and ensuring tenant satisfaction year-round. At Mursen Maintenance, we've seen how proactive care prevents the vast majority of emergency repair calls.

## Spring: The Refresh Season

**1. Gutter Cleaning & Inspection**

Spring rains can overwhelm clogged gutters, leading to foundation damage and basement flooding. After winter debris积累, gutters need thorough cleaning and inspection for damage.

- Remove all debris from gutters and downspouts
- Check for proper slope and secure attachments
- Inspect for rust, cracks, or separation
- Ensure downspouts extend at least 6 feet from foundation

**2. HVAC System Tune-Up**

Before summer heat arrives, schedule an HVAC inspection. A well-maintained system is 10–15% more efficient and lasts significantly longer.

- Replace air filters
- Clean condenser coils
- Check refrigerant levels
- Inspect ductwork for leaks
- Test thermostat accuracy

## Summer: Peak Usage Season

**3. Exterior Inspection & Repair**

Summer storms can cause wear. Walk your property's perimeter and check:

- Roof for missing or damaged shingles
- Foundation for cracks or settling
- Siding and trim for damage
- Window and door seals
- Deck and patio conditions

**4. Termite & Pest Prevention**

Warm weather brings unwanted guests. A proactive pest management plan includes:

- Remove wood-to-soil contact around foundation
- Store firewood at least 20 feet from building
- Trim tree branches away from roof
- Seal foundation cracks and gaps
- Schedule professional termite inspection

## Fall: Winter Preparation

**5. Winterization Checklist**

Before freezing temperatures hit, prepare:

- Drain outdoor faucets and irrigation systems
- Install faucet covers
- Check insulation in attics and crawl spaces
- Service furnace and fireplace
- Test smoke and carbon monoxide detectors
- Reverse ceiling fans to clockwise rotation
